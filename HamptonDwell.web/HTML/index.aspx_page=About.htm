Headline: About Us

24 May 2012

Create, from the imagination of our customers, a high end beach/country living environment, with the elegance, luxury and comfort typically experimented in this heavenly resort called "The Hamptons".

 

We are a synergy of multidisciplinary teams with a common goal which is to provide the highest and most comfortable beach living that elements and human design combined can provide, always committed to develop according to the carefullest construction codes and the highest craftsmanship techniques.

 

Our team is conformed for talented designers, as well as the ancient techniques of craftsman from several parts of the globe experienced in manipulate materials such wood, stone and vegetation; as new projects have been appearing, new opportunities of challenge our abilities to modify the nature sources and incorporate them into vanguard techniques to generate beauty, restore heritage and provide the most exquisite home environment indoor and outdoors.
